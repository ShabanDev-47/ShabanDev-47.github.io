Circles
"The project questions the VR technology: Is it a technology devoid of sentiments? Can we deliver spiritual messages through it? People might disagree, even argue, about the questions aforementioned, however, we can all agree on the fact that technology is to take over the future. What is to become of us shall we not be able to express our genuine selves in this foreseen future? Will that technology-run world render us emotionless?" -Artist Statment

During my collaboration with medrar for contemporary art, I participated & helped the Artists with development of more than 6 projects / VR experiences for "Fantomas Lab".
Fantomas is a VR exhibtion hosted by Medrar for contemporary art in collaboration with 412 labs.

This VR experience "Circles" was selected to participate in Fourth edition of Art Cairo at the Grand Egyptian Museum
